About & Ministry Vision

My Calling & Journey

Answering the divine call to ministry at the pivotal age of 19, Bishop John's journey began with a clear mission to shepherd and transform lives. He planted his very first congregation in Wikililye, Kitui County, Kenya, laying a foundation of faith and community development that would grow for decades.

Led to expand this assignment, he later transitioned to Kitui Town, where he faithfully ministers to this day as the visionary leader of the Transformation Information Centre.

Leadership & Impact

Driven by a profound calling to equip the next generation of Christian leaders, he has established several thriving church branches across Kenya. He is also a published author, having penned several books dedicated to organizational and spiritual leadership keys.

As the founder of the Arise Africa Movement, his ministry extends beyond the local church walls to inspire continental revival, strategic leadership empowerment, and social transformation.

Ministry Philosophy

His ministry is built on the pillars of expository teaching, holistic transformation, and servant leadership. He believes the church should not only be a place of spiritual worship but an information and empowerment center that equips believers to impact every sphere of society—marketplace, family, and governance.

Theological Foundation

True leadership requires deep scriptural grounding. Over his decades of ministry, he has diligently attended and completed several advanced, intensive Bible studies and theological courses. This commitment to continuous spiritual education ensures his teachings remain deeply rooted in sound doctrine, hermeneutical accuracy, and practical wisdom for modern living.
